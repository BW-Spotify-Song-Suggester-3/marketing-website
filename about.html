ShadowWav

We’ve all been there. Trying to find new music and being offered up the same songs over and over again. Frustrating to say the least.

Well we’ve taken it upon ourselves to offer you a solution. Here we offer not only a more sophisticated way to discover music based on your favorite songs and current mood, but also a way to visualize your favorite tunes’ similarities and differences.

Purpose

  • To provide fresh tunes to new ears.
  • To give users an effective tool to discover music based on their tastes.
  • To help users find and visualize songs that fit their personal taste.

Features

  • Discover music based on mood.
  • Discover music based on your favorites (save your faves with us!).
  • The app also identifies songs with similar audio features.
  • A user can search for a specific song and see its audio features displayed in a visually appealing way.
